Pietential

Realize Your Growth Potential


Pietential is based on Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs. Through the free online survey (which takes 5-7 minutes to complete) the Pietential learning platform instantly creates a pie chart and bar graph that helps you Visualize and Analyze where you stand regarding the core issues that are central to what it is to be human: Physiological Needs, Safety Needs, Love and Belonging, Esteem, and Self-actualization. And it offers observations, recommendations and coaching towards living your best life.

And Pietential is free!

PietentialPRO

Track and Share your Progress with PietentialPRO


PietentialPRO is for individual Pietential Subscribers.

It allows you to set reminders to retake Pietential at your convenience and it automatically tracks your progress each time you log in by line graphing your journey towards improved wellbeing over time.

And, PietentialPRO allows you to share your Pietential results on social media platforms in order to let your friends know how you’re doing, where you might need their support - and the progress you’re making towards being the best version of yourself.

PietentialADVISOR

Build Trust More Quickly and Stay Connected with PietentialADVISOR
-OR-

PietentialADVISOR is for life coaches, nutritionist and personal trainers; CEO Mastermind group facilitators and business consultants; CVAs, exit planners, and business brokers - people who are in the business of adviaing others! It helps you build trust more quickly by getting into a conversation with clients (and prospective clients) about about life balance, and helps you stay better connected to your clients while understanding  what’s important to them.
 
PietentialADVISOR Subscribers are able to issue PietentialPro seat licenses (with all accompanying features and benefits) to their clients and prospective clients, and can monitor their well-being through the PietentialADVISOR Advisor Dashboard.  Moreover, ADVISOR Subscribers can set alerts related to their client or prospective client’s wellbeing which notify the advisor if a given person’s scores have fallen below a certain threshold set by you, the Advisor.
 
For ADVISORs who wish to issue seat licenses to prospective clients for business development purposes, the PietentialADVISOR Platform allows ADVISORs to export their Pietential contacts into their CRM or sales force automation software for use as part of their nurture marketing campaigns.

PietentialBusiness

Support Employee Wellbeing with PietentialBUSINESS
-OR-

The PietentialBUSINESS Platform allows owners of small and medium businesses, and directors of nonprofit organizations to offer PietentialPro (with all of its features and benefits) to all of their employees in order to support employee wellbeing on an individual, or an aggregate basis. 
 
Pietential Business offers a customizable front-end employee survey which allows organizational leadership to visualize and analyze employee wellbeing by location, job title, age, or any other demographic factors that their human resource department keeps track of.

PietentialENTERPRISE

Visualize Population Wellbeing with PietnetialENTERPRISE
-OR-

The “PietentialENTERPRISE” population wellbeing monitoring platform is for organizations with large population (often de-centralized populations) like national and global corporations, School Systems; Colleges, Universities and University Systems; large NPOs and NGOs; and Local, State and Federal Agencies who want to be able to to visualize, quantify and better understand their population wellbeing in order that they can take steps to improve it.
 
PietentialENTERPRISE affords every member of the organization a PietentialPRO license and the ability to visualize their own personal well being.  And, through a custom API integration it gives the organization’s leadership the ability to visualize the wellbeing of the entire organization in aggregate, or to view any number of comparative visualizations segmented in any manner in which the client’s HR database is already architected.
