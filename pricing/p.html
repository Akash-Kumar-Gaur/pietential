Pietential

Track and Share your Progress with Pietential


Pietential is “The Life Balance Realization System”

Pietential is for individuals who want to be able to visualize their current life balance, receive feedback and observations on their current state of being, and take action towards a more fulfilled, more balanced life.

It allows you to set reminders to retake Pietential at your convenience, so you can “Visualize it. Analyze it. And Realize it.” any time you like. And it automatically tracks your progress each time you log in, by line graphing your journey towards improved wellbeing over time.

And, Pietential allows you to share your Pietential results on social media platforms in order to let your friends know how you’re doing, where you might need their support - and the progress you’re making towards being the best version of yourself.

Designed for People who are in the Business of Advising Others!

PietentialADVISOR is for people who serve as a trusted advisor to their clients (and prospective clients): life coaches, counselors, therapists, financial advisors, nutritionist and personal trainers; CEO Mastermind group facilitators and business consultants; CVAs, exit planners, business brokers, and other types of professional advisors can use it to monitor the well being of the people they advise, and to get and stay more deeply connected to them.

And, when used as a business development tool, it helps you build trust more quickly by getting into a conversation with prospective clients about life balance, and what’s important to them.

PietentialBusiness

Support Employee Wellbeing with PietentialBUSINESS

The PietentialBUSINESS Platform allows owners of small and medium businesses, and directors of nonprofit organizations to offer Pietential (with all of its features and benefits) to all of their employees in order to support employee wellbeing on an individual, or an aggregate basis.
Pietential Business offers a customizable front-end employee survey which allows organizational leadership to visualize and analyze employee wellbeing by location, job title, age, or any other demographic factors that their human resource department keeps track of.
User settings allow company employees to choose whether or not share their Pietential status with their employer's HR Department and/or ownership. In any case, the company receives aggregate visualizations, showing the well being of the employee population as charts without revealing individual employees' status.

PietentialENTERPRISE

Visualize Population Wellbeing with PietnetialENTERPRISE
-OR-

The “PietentialENTERPRISE” population wellbeing monitoring platform is for organizations with large population (often de-centralized populations) like national and global corporations, School Systems; Colleges, Universities and University Systems; large NPOs and NGOs; and Local, State and Federal Agencies who want to be able to to visualize, quantify and better understand their population wellbeing in order that they can take steps to improve it.
PietentialENTERPRISE affords every member of the organization a Pietential license and the ability to visualize their own personal well being.  And, through a custom API integration it gives the organization’s leadership the ability to visualize the wellbeing of the entire organization in aggregate, or to view any number of comparative visualizations segmented in any manner in which the client’s HR database is already architected.
