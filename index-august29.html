Pietential™

The Life Balance & Population Wellbeing Realization System

Pietential is Based on Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs.

It's for People who want to Grow

At the individual user level, Pietential is a simple survey that helps you Visualize, and Analyze where you stand regarding the core issues that are central to what it is to be human: Physiological Needs, Safety Needs, Love and Belonging, Esteem, and Self-actualization.

Scores are rendered in an easy to read pie chart, and observations and recommendations are offered instantly, so you can work towards realizing your best life. You can log back in any time and track your progress. Unless you choose to share your Pietential it's kept confidential. Best of all it's completely free for individual users, in alignment with our Vision, Mission and Values.

And Organizations Investing in Human Potential

For advisors, small businesses & nonprofit organizations, large enterprises and NGOs that license Pietential, it's a realtime emotional index. Pietential provides your organization valuable aggregated comparative data visualizations of the emotional wellbeing of different segments of populations you lead and serve, allowing you to better understand how your people are doing.
