Track and Share your Progress with Pietential


Pietential is “The Life Balance Realization System”

Pietential is for individuals who want to be able to visualize their current life balance, receive feedback and observations on their current state of being, and take action towards a more fulfilled, more balanced life.

It allows you to set reminders to retake Pietential at your convenience, so you can “Visualize it. Analyze it. And Realize it.” any time you like. And it automatically tracks your progress each time you log in, by line graphing your journey towards improved wellbeing over time.

And, Pietential allows you to share your Pietential results on social media platforms in order to let your friends know how you’re doing, where you might need their support - and the progress you’re making towards being the best version of yourself.

The PietentialBUSINESS Platform allows owners of small and medium businesses, and directors of nonprofit organizations to offer Pietential (with all of its features and benefits) to all of their employees in order to support employee wellbeing on an individual, or an aggregate basis.

PietentialENTERPRISE

Visualize Population Wellbeing with PietnetialENTERPRISE
-OR-

The “PietentialENTERPRISE” population wellbeing monitoring platform is for organizations with large population (often de-centralized populations) like national and global corporations, School Systems; Colleges, Universities and University Systems; large NPOs and NGOs; and Local, State and Federal Agencies who want to be able to to visualize, quantify and better understand their population wellbeing in order that they can take steps to improve it.
PietentialENTERPRISE affords every member of the organization a Pietential license and the ability to visualize their own personal well being.  And, through a custom API integration it gives the organization’s leadership the ability to visualize the wellbeing of the entire organization in aggregate, or to view any number of comparative visualizations segmented in any manner in which the client’s HR database is already architected.
 

Pietential™

The Life Balance & Population Wellbeing Realization System

Pietential is Based on Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs.

It’s for People who want to Grow

At the individual user level, Pietential is a simple survey that helps you Visualize, and Analyze where you stand regarding the core issues that are central to what it is to be human: Physiological Needs, Safety Needs, Love and Belonging, Esteem, and Self-actualization.

Scores are rendered in an easy to read pie chart, and observations and recommendations are offered instantly, so you can work towards realizing your best life. You can log back in any time and track your progress. Unless you choose to share your Pietential it’s kept confidential. Best of all it’s completely free for individual users, in alignment with our Vision, Mission and Values.

And Organizations Investing in Human Potential

For advisors, small businesses & nonprofit organizations, large enterprises and NGOs that license Pietential, it’s a realtime emotional index. Pietential provides your organization valuable aggregated comparative data visualizations of the emotional wellbeing of different segments of populations you lead and serve, allowing you to better understand how your people are doing.

About Us

Our Vision

Our Vision is that of a more balanced, more equitable world, where every person and every organization has the opportunity to understand and realize their full potential.

Our Mission

Pietential is on a Mission to bring its Life Balance Realization System to the world. Every day we wake up and share this opportunity for personal, organizational, community, and global growth.

Our Values

Pietential is a democratizing force for personal, organizational, community and global well-being.


Pietential empowers people to Visualize their current state of being, Analyze ideas and actions towards improvement, and Realize their human and/or organizational potential by monitoring progress towards the life and organizational balance they envision.

Our Team

Pietential, LLC is headquartered in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania “The birthplace of America” and was founded by John Starling and Henry “Hank” Mitchell in 2019, and we’re on a mission to bring our “Life Balance Realization System” to the world, empowering people and organizations to understand and realize their full potential. And, as a company with a double bottom line we are committed to being a democratizing force for people and communities around the world that can’t afford professional coaching.


Many of our team members are based in India, the world’s largest democracy - and one of the world’s great cradles of wisdom. Please take a moment to get to know our team. To know them is to love them.


We’re looking to expand our team in the U.S., India, and around the world. If you’d like to join our team, please contact us.
