About Us

The Life Balance Realization System™

Based on Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs, Pietential helps you Visualize, and Analyze where you stand regarding the core issues that are central to what it is to be human: Physiological Needs, Safety Needs, Love and Belonging, Esteem, and Self-actualization, so you can Realize your best life.

Our Vision

Our Vision is that of a more balanced, more equitable world, where every person and every organization has the opportunity to understand and realize their full potential.

Our Mission

Pietential is on a Mission to bring its Life Balance Realization System to the world. Every day we wake up and share this opportunity for personal, organizational, community, and global growth.

Our Values

Pietential is a democratizing force for personal, organizational, community and global well-being.

Pietential empowers people to Visualize their current state of being, Analyze ideas and actions towards improvement, and Realize their human and/or organizational potential by monitoring progress towards the life and organizational balance they envision.

Our Story

Our Team

Pietential, LLC is headquartered in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania “The birthplace of America” and was founded by John Starling and Henry “Hank” Mitchell in 2019, and we’re on a mission to bring our “Life Balance Realization System” to the world, empowering people and organizations to understand and realize their full potential. And, as a company with a double bottom line we are committed to being a democratizing force for people and communities around the world that can’t afford professional coaching.

Many of our team members are based in India, the world’s largest democracy - and one of the world’s great cradles of wisdom. Please take a moment to get to know our team. To know them is to love them.

We’re looking to expand our team in the U.S., India, and around the world. If you’d like to join our team, please contact us. We’re always looking for bright, driven people who want to improve business culture, life balance and results, and change their communities (and the world for the better ) one person at a time.
