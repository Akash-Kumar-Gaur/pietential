John Starling

Chief Executive Officer

John Starling has dedicated his life to helping people define and achieve what's important to them and is the creator of "Pietential".
Since 1999 he's owned and operated several management consulting firms and has been a trusted business advisor and coach to nearly 400 companies (from start-ups to Fortune 50 companies) and countless individuals.
He is currently in private practice as a professional coach (Starling Growth Advisory) and leads CEO peer advisory groups for Learner Leader Cohorts.
In 2010 he founded a first of its kind MMA (Mentoring & Martial Arts) school for low-income, at-risk youth in Baltimore.
In 2016 he founded a girls' soccer club for elementary-aged school girls in South Korea who had no opportunity to play organized sports in their schools due to institutional and cultural gender discrimination.

Jonathan Hanna

Product Development Lead

Jonathan Hanna is a student and observer of human and non-human nature, dedicated to impacting wider audiences positively. He draws on more than 30 years of studying consciousness in Eastern and Western traditions, expanding his understanding of the living book of wisdom called Life. Having experienced the philosophies of Indian ashrams, Jonathan seeks to incorporate his learning into his career.
He resonates with the philosophy behind Pietential's embrace of Abraham Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs, aligning him with the company's purpose.
Jonathan is a husband, and father to a young daughter, seeking to do as little harm as possible to the Earth and all sentient beings. This philosophy of compassion is foundational to his service work.
Jonathan is the Product Development Lead at Pietential and is directly responsible for the ongoing improvement of the company's product and how it intersects and empowers people and organizations worldwide.
