Pietential is a Life Balance and Population Wellbeing Realization survey tool. It empowers people and communities to Visualize their current state of being, Analyze ideas and actions toward improvement, and Realize their human and community potential.

Our social mission is to create a more balanced world by offering a wellbeing assessment and coaching platform to every individual in the world.

Pietential empowers NGOs and NPOs to visualize and monitor the wellbeing of the communities they serve.

Who are our partners?

Who are our partners?

For a mission of this scale, we are partnering with NGOs and NPOs worldwide through our PietentialNGO platform. It is specially curated to amplify the impact of organizations that are doing so much good in the world.

Pietential helps such organizations understand, monitor, and support the wellbeing of the communities they serve, and helps prove the efficacy of their initiatives.
