Pietential is a Wellbeing Visualization Tool and Personal Development Platform

We created it by reverse-engineering Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs into an online wellbeing assessment. It lets you Visualize, Analyze, Realize, and Actualize your state of being and growth potential. Pietential measures and monitors your personal development progress while offering observations, recommendations, and self improvement exercises to foster your personal growth.

Pietential

BUSINESS

Choosing from over 50 demographic categories, SMBs can monitor and compare employee wellbeing, while offering personal growth support.

The Pietential BUSINESS Platform allows owners of small and medium businesses (SMBs) to offer Pietential to their employees in order to support employee wellbeing on an individual basis, and better understand employee population wellbeing on an aggregate basis through data visualizations.

Pietential BUSINESS does not require that you have an HRIS system (that’s Pietential Enterprise). “Business” offers a front-end employee demographic survey with over 50 predefined categories. It gives organizational leadership the ability to visualize and analyze employee wellbeing by location, job title, age, or any other demographic factors that your human resource department keeps track of. And, give you the ability to customize the survey, and download reports as CSV and XML files.

System settings allow company employees to choose whether or not share their individual Pietential status with their HR Department, management and company ownership, and the company always receives anonymized aggregate visualizations showing the well being of the employee population as charts, without revealing individual employees’ status.

Pietential NGO includes observations and recommendations for the individuals that the NGO administers it to, as well as Pietential’s “Actualize It” personal growth exercises, but NGOs also have the option to turn off our wellbeing programs in order to specifically measure the effectiveness of their own programs. This means that NGOs now have the power to prove their value to their funders!

Our Process

How we do it

User admin
  • Submit your Admin Subscription request.

    Pietential Admin services are customized based on your organization and the populations you lead and serve. So, the query form is designed to help us better understand your requirements.

  • We will contact you within 48 hours to schedule a meeting.

    Once we receive your request, our team will schedule a call with you. It’s important for us to understand your potential use case for the Pietential platform so that we can help you be successful with it.

  • We will give you a detailed demo and share the terms to be signed.

    After both parties agree and the terms are signed, we will invoice you for the payment. Shortly after, you will receive a link to register your Admin account. You can always book onboarding support calls if needed.

  • Customize your demographic categories.

    Every organization and population is different; based on your choices, we will help you build a customized demographic profile for your population. These demographics will later be used to draw a comparative analysis.

  • Receive a organization code to onboard your population.

    Once you have set up your Admin account, you will receive a organization code that you can share with your population so they can use the platform.

  • Familiarize yourself with the Admin dashboard.

    Log in with your active Admin Id and start using your customized Admin dashboard. If you need any support, you can book a Zoom call with us.

  • Invite new users and Monitor your population’s wellbeing.

    You can invite new users into your population by uploading contacts manually or via XML/CSV files, or you can use our custom API. The “Population Average” feature lets you monitor and track your entire population’s wellbeing.

  • Create, Compare, and Analyze different individuals, cohorts, and demographics within your population.

    You can create different segmented cohorts within your population. The “Comparative Analysis” feature lets you compare and analyze the wellbeing of different cohorts, demographics, and individuals.

  • Sign up as a user.

    Fill in your details, and you are ready to experience Pietential. If you have a organization code, you will receive an additional form regarding the demographic details requested by your Admin.

  • Complete a simple survey.

    Complete a 5-7 minutes survey to evaluate your current state of life balance and wellbeing. Be authentic; it’s not a test. There are no right or wrong answers to these questions.

  • Receive a Visualization of your life balance.

    Once you answer all the questions, you will receive a visualization of your life balance in the form of a pie chart and bar graph.

  • Get instant observations, recommendations, and personal growth-hacking exercises.

    Based on your current wellbeing scores, Pietential provides instant observations, recommendations, and personal growth-hacking exercises to help you take your wellbeing to the next level.

  • Track your progress and personal growth.

    You can retake the Pietential survey as many times as you want. Simply log in and click on retake the survey. Once you have taken survey more than once, you will see a line graph of your life balance which will help you track your progress.

  • And it is completely Free!

    Pietential is on a Mission to bring its Life Balance Realization System to the world. To achieve that, our Individual Subscription is completely free!
